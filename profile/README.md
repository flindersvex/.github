## Flinders VEX Robotics Team

Passionate about robotics, engineering, or VEX competitions? The Flinders VEX Team is a welcoming community for students keen to explore robotics, electronics, and hands-on STEM challenges. Whether you're a seasoned builder or just getting started, you'll have opportunities to design, build, and compete with robots at local, national, and even international levels. Join us to gain practical skills, make new friends, and represent Flinders University in the exciting world of competitive robotics!

Check out [our website](https://www.flindersvex.com) to join our club and community. We'd love to have you!
